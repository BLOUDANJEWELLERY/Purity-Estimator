How to Measure Properly (Important!):

• Weigh the object dry and record the value.
• Zero the scale with a water-filled container on it.
• Suspend the gold object using thread or fine wire.
• Fully submerge it without letting it touch the bottom or sides.
• Ensure your hand or holder stays outside the water.
• Record the increase in weight shown on the scale — this is the displaced water weight (equals volume in cm³).
