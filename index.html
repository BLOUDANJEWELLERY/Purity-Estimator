Gold Purity Estimator

Weight-Based Water Displacement

How to Measure Dry Weight:
Use a precise scale to measure the weight of your gold object. Ensure it is clean and dry.
How to Measure Water Displacement:
Zero the scale with container of water on it. Suspend your gold object in water and note the weight on the scale. This is the displaced water weight.
How to Measure Properly (Important!):

• Weigh the gold object dry.
• Zero the scale with the container.
• Suspend the object fully in water without touching the sides.
• Record the increase in weight (this is the displaced water weight in grams).
• Avoid touching the water or letting the object rest on the container.
